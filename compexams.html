  • On AIR's Balochi service
    Why in news

    On Sept-16, the All India Radio (AIR) launched a website and mobile app for its Balochi service to reach people who speak this language across the globe.
    About Balochi language -
  • It is a north-western Iranian language.
  • It is the principal language of the Baloch people, who live mainly in the Balochistan region of the southeastern-most edge of the Iranian plateau in Pakistan, Iran, and Afghanistan, as well as in the Arabian Peninsula.
  • The mobile-app and the webpage were launched just as “value addition” to AIR's Balochi service, which has been in existence since 1974.
  • The External Services Division (ESD) of AIR presently broadcasts daily in 57 radio transmissions covering over 108 countries in 27 languages.
