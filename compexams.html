  • Pusa Basmati 1979 & Pusa Basmati 1985 launched

    These are herbicide tolerant and non-GM varieties of paddy developed by PUSA.
    Features
  • Contains acetolactate synthase (ALS) gene whose DNA has been modified using chemical mutant, Ethyl Methanaosulphate thereby preventing Imazethapr from killing the plant
  • Use: Labour and water-intensive paddy transplantation can be done away with. Currently, water is the natural herbicide that takes care of weeds in the paddy's early growth period
  • Direct Seeding of Rice have been adopted in Punjab and Haryana in response to labour shortages. These farmers can now spray on paddy, Imazethapr
  • About Imazethapr- a broad-spectrum herbicide to control broadleaf, grassy and sedge weeds; does not distinguish from crop and invasive plants
  • Acetolactate Synthase (ALS) gene is a gene that codes for enzyme for a protein that synthesises amino acids for crop growth and development

  • On Goa's Feni Policy, 2021

    On August 12, 2021, the Government of Goa notified the "Goa Feni Policy", 2021 with the vision of promoting feni as a World Heritage Drink.
    About Feni -
  • Feni is a distilled alcoholic beverage made from cashew apple or from coconut nectar.
  • Cashew Feni was given Geographical Indication status in 2007.
  • Goa: Is located on the Konkan coast; Separated from Deccan highlands by Western Ghats
